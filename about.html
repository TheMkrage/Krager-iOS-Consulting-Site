Idea Development

Have the beginnings of an idea for an app? I can help you further develop your idea by identifying competition, determining technical feasibility, and connecting you to others in related markets. Getting off the Ground

Confident in your idea and ready to build an app? I can give you the best practices for forming and managing an iOS development team, especially for those with non-technical background. I can also give tips on finding and evaluating potential hires for iOS development.

The iOS ecosystem is quite unique and I can help you avoid common pitfalls and save your company hours of time when ramping up development.

Starting Development on an existing codebase

Did your company purchase or outsource an existing app or codebase? It can be difficult to navigate and decide on the best next steps. Posting on the App Store

There are many guidelines and regulations for what you can and can't do and it's important to know before you spend any unnecessary resources.
